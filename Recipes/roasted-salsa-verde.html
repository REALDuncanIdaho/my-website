Roasted Salsa Verde

Description

There is a place beyond time, beyond reason, beyond doubt, where human consiousness ceases to be. All of our failures and fears are reduced to nothing. All that remains is pure flavor. Remove your mask, embrace who you were always meant to be. Let this salsa recipe be your vessel to a new version of yourself.

Requirements

  • broiler
  • food processor/blender

Ingredients

  • tomatillos, medium sized, 6-8
  • white onion, 1
  • serrano peppers, 2-4
  • garlic, as much as you like
  • cilantro
  • honey, 1-2tbps
  • salt, 1-2tps

Steps

  1. Preheat broiler to high heat. Place peeled and washed tomatillos on baking tray, recommend covering in aluminum foil for easy clean up, then place in broiler. Cook until tops are blackened, then flip tomatillos and return until reverse is the same. 10-15 minutes.

  2. Separate garlic cloves while leaving the skin on. Dice the onion. Add onions, garlic, and serrano peppers to pan over medium heat. Cook until onion has carmalized, just before blackening. Garlic and Peppers should have a light char. Once done let cool slightly and deskin the garlic. Cut off the stems of the peppers, can dice peppers to save your food processor trouble.

  3. Add tomatillos, onion, peppers, and garlic to food processor. Chop cilantro and add, along with honey and salt. Blend well, if not eating immediately store in refridgerator. Enjoy!
